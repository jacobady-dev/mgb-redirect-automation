Mapping assumptions
  • Manual exclude URLs are copied to their own tab and removed from automated processing.
  • Provider pages are mapped to the IA provider URL unless manually mapped 1:1.
  • Hospital-level pages map to the hospital-specific /en/about/[hospital] URLs from the IA workbook.
  • Service, patient/visitor, medical professional, education, notices, and giving destinations use IA workbook Full URL patterns.
  • Assets are separated into JS/CSS, images, document assets, videos, and excluded non-HTML.
