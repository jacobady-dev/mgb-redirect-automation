Mapping assumptions
  • Manual exclude URLs are copied to their own tab and removed from automated processing.
  • Provider detail pages are sent to HUMAN CHECK unless a confirmed provider destination is supplied later.
  • Location pages map only when a strong destination match exists.
  • News, article, press release, and historical update pages are conservatively flagged for HUMAN CHECK.
  • Assets are separated into JS/CSS, images, document assets, videos, and excluded non-HTML.
